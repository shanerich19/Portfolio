ABOUT THIS

Import Apparel is a fictitious shopping website for car enthuiests. The goal with this project was to create something that would feel familiar to the demographic. I took lots of inspiration from other websites and made my own based on the common themes I saw. The website consists of a homepage, followed by three category pages, a shop page, and a cart. Each category has 6 items with their own description and related products section.
