ABOUT THIS

Suga Cube is a made up association that provides medical supplies to Type 1 Diabetics who cannot afford them. This was a project of passion as I am also diabetic so it was cool to design something for a demographic that I'm a part of (ages 16-40). The logo is a cube with a drop of blood inside because diabetics have to take a drop of blood at least 4 times a day to check their blood sugar.
