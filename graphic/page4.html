Tesla Ad

ABOUT THIS

Tesla is a company that has no print advertisements, so I made some.These started off with an image of the new Model 3 and a plain background. Tesla is a very youthful company that loves wordplay so I designed both ads with those aspects in mind. The first ad shows the Model 3 with an antenna as a joke because the car can be controlled with an app similar to a remote controlled car, and the second ad is meant to confuse the reader at first by taking common battery driven items and labeling them as gas driven.
