    ABOUT DESIGNING

    When I begin any project I always start off with mental drawings. I take those mental drawnings and make them either digital or physical depending on what I find gives the best results. The next part of the process is experimentation; playing with the colors, the shapes, sizes, layout, until I have a large selection of ideas to choose from. The best ideas get polished and used in the product presented. After critique both by myself and by the client, I go back and make the suggested changes until the product is complete. Designing is all trial and error.
