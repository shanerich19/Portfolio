G-Shock Brochure

ABOUT THIS

For this project I set out to design a brochure for G-Shock watches that was cohesive with their current branding. To portray their philosophy, I photographed their watches in enviornments that show the watches capabilities, also stated on the brochure. Other highlights include a 2 page features section and company philosophy section.
